THE FUTURE SHOULD WARN YOU FIRST.

> Predict. Protect. Prosper.

PLUTUS is an autonomous multi-agent intelligence platform designed to predict and prevent financial and healthcare crises before they happen.

Instead of reacting to emergencies after they occur, PLUTUS continuously analyzes health, finance, insurance, and fraud signals to identify hidden risks and generate proactive intervention plans.

---

## The Problem

Every year millions of people face devastating financial hardship triggered by preventable health events.

Health systems understand illness.

Financial systems understand money.

Insurance systems understand policies.

Nobody understands the domino effect between them.

---

## Our Solution

PLUTUS creates a unified risk intelligence layer powered by autonomous AI agents.

Each specialized agent focuses on a critical domain:

🏥 Health Agent

💰 Financial Agent

🛡️ Insurance Agent

🔒 Fraud Detection Agent

Together they collaborate to generate a real-time Crisis Probability Index and personalized prevention strategies.
